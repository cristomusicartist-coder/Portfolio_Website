About me

My name is Cristian Giordano, a musician with 16 years of experience. To me, sound is a powerful language capable of creating entire worlds with just a few notes. I specialize in crafting immersive soundscapes where music production and sound design merge to tell stories and create unforgettable experiences.

Currently, I am actively working on my long-term project: "Film Made Only By Music" - a series of EPs where the listener is immersed in a complete story created entirely through sound and music.
